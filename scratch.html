My favorite dogs are pugs! Here's why:

  1. Wrinkles
    • Pugs are super-duper wrinkly.
    • When you pet them, you can scratch them between their fat rolls.
  2. Legs
    • These dogs have such short legs.
    • Sometimes if they are really fat, their belly drags on the floor due to how close their legs allow their body to be to the ground.
  3. Pointless
    • Pugs don't really contribute a lot to society.
    • I like this idea because they are just available at all times for cuteness.
